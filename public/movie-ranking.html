The Best Movies According To Murli

My top 3 movies of all time

Grave of the fireflies

This is one of the most beautiful animation movie I have ever seen. This movie basically focus on the struggles of brother and sister who are in the middle of world war in japan and have no one to rely on so they take care of each other. It's story is very emotional. A must watch for sure.

Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama

Again another animation movie.This is by far the best animated adaptation of Ramayana. The songs are very beautiful too. This movie is made by a both Indian and Japanese animator. The story follows Ramayana. Again Must watch for sure.

The Pianist

This movie shows the struggles of a Polish-Jewish pianist who do everything to stay alive during the world war. Amazing Story, The story shows how his artistic gift helps in survival. Again Must watch.
